Welcome to Analogics Group

Today, Analogics has a broad portfolio of 50+ products and solutions catering to various needs in multiple sectors such as utilities, transport, banking, solar, defence, police and security Analogics also provides products and solutions for Time and Attendance, Spot Billing and Ticketing, and Retail Billing applications.

Since its founding in 1994, Analogics has focussed on being at the forefront of technological advancements, adapting to global industry trends, and understanding the evolving needs of the customer in order to create innovative products and solutions.

Infrastructure

Analogics is an ISO 9001:2008 and ISO 14001:2004 certified company. It has also achieved the Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI) Level 3 certification for process improvement. The Company is also a Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR) Recognised R&D Center.

Leadership Management

Mr. Surender Reddy

Founder and Managing Director

Mr. Surender Reddy, who has over 20 years of experience in the electronic industry, is the driving force behind Analogics ever since the company’s founding. His mission has been to make Analogics a technology-focussed, product-oriented organization conforming to the highest standards of quality and research. He began his career in 1978 as an engineer with the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

Mr. B Maruthy

Director, Research and Development

Mr. Maruthy, who has more than 25 years of experience in the electronic industry, Analogics has created and launched innovative products and solutions for the energy and utilities segments. He began his career in 1981 as an Engineer Assistant with the Research and Development (R&D) department of Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL), an enterprise of the Government of India.
